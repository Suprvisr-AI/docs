## Opening Settings

Click the **Settings (gear) icon** in the bottom-left sidebar to open the Settings panel.

---

## Appearance

### Accent color

Choose from four accent colors that apply throughout the app:

| Color | Name |
|---|---|
| 🟠 | **Coral** (default) |
| 🔵 | **Azure** |
| 🟢 | **Emerald** |
| 🟡 | **Amber** |

### Theme

| Option | What it does |
|---|---|
| **System** | Follows your operating system's light/dark preference |
| **Light** | Always light mode |
| **Dark** | Always dark mode |

## Personal info

Under **Personalization**, you can update:

- **First name** — how the AI addresses you in conversation
- **Last name**
- **About you** — a short bio that helps the AI personalize its responses

<Callout type="info">
  Your preferred name appears in the greeting on the empty chat screen: "Hello [name], what do you want to tackle today?"
</Callout>

---

## Your role

Your role was set during onboarding, but you can change it at any time.

<Steps>

### Go to Settings → Personalization

Click the gear icon in the sidebar, then select the Personalization tab.

### Select a new role

Choose from Developer / Engineer, Product Manager, Designer / Marketer, or Other.

### Changes apply immediately

New chats will use your updated role for response framing and example prompts.

</Steps>

## Redo onboarding

Want to walk through the full onboarding flow again?

1. Go to **Settings → Personalization**
2. Click **Redo Onboarding**
3. Complete the 3-step flow (name → role → features tour)

<Callout type="info">
  Redoing onboarding updates your name and role. Your chat history and cards are not affected.
</Callout>
