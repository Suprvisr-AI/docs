# Features

Suprvisr AI is a full-featured AI workspace built for teams. Here's everything it can do.

## Core AI

<Cards>
  <Cards.Card title="AI Chat" href="/features/ai-chat" arrow>
    Streaming responses, role-aware answers, example prompts, and stop generation.
  </Cards.Card>
  <Cards.Card title="Reasoning & Verbosity" href="/features/reasoning-verbosity" arrow>
    Multi-level reasoning for deep answers. Verbosity modes from quick to verbose.
  </Cards.Card>
  <Cards.Card title="Dictate" href="/features/dictate" arrow>
    Speak your messages with the mic icon. Hands-free, voice-to-text input.
  </Cards.Card>
</Cards>

## Workspace

<Cards>
  <Cards.Card title="Workspaces" href="/features/workspaces" arrow>
    Project folders with shared AI context. Invite teammates, thread conversations.
  </Cards.Card>
  <Cards.Card title="Conversations" href="/features/conversations" arrow>
    Pin, search, delete, and organize your chat history.
  </Cards.Card>
  <Cards.Card title="Cards & Artifacts" href="/features/cards" arrow>
    AI-generated documents and lists that persist alongside your chats.
  </Cards.Card>
  <Cards.Card title="Citations" href="/features/citations" arrow>
    Source cards for every answer that draws from your internal data.
  </Cards.Card>
  <Cards.Card title="File Uploads" href="/features/file-uploads" arrow>
    Drag-and-drop files for instant AI analysis. Supports docs, images, and data.
  </Cards.Card>
</Cards>
