# SharePoint Integration

Connect Suprvisr to your Microsoft SharePoint document libraries. Once connected, the AI can reference, search, and answer questions about your organization's documents directly — no manual uploads required.

## What it does

With SharePoint connected, Suprvisr can:

- **Search your documents** for relevant information when you ask a question
- **Reference specific files** without you having to upload them manually
- **Show sources** — when the AI uses a SharePoint document, it shows the source alongside the response
- **Stay in sync** — your document library is periodically synced so the AI has fresh content

---

## Setting up SharePoint

<Steps>

### Go to Settings → Integrations

Click **Settings** (gear icon) → select the **Integrations** tab → click **SharePoint**.

### Connect your site

Click **Add Connection** and authenticate with your Microsoft account. Grant the required permissions to access your SharePoint sites.

### Select a document library

Choose which SharePoint site and document library (drive) to sync. You can add multiple sites.

### Wait for the initial sync

Suprvisr syncs your documents in the background. The sync status shows the last sync time and document count.

</Steps>

---

## Sync status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Connected and syncing normally |
| **Syncing** | Currently processing documents |
| **Error** | Sync failed — check the error message |

Click **Refresh** to manually trigger a sync at any time.

## Using SharePoint in chat

Once connected, just ask naturally — the AI searches your SharePoint content automatically when relevant:

- "Does our employee handbook cover remote work policies?"
- "What does our Q3 sales report say about Canada?"
- "Find the latest version of our service agreement template"

<Callout type="tip">
  Be specific about what you're looking for. The AI searches by relevance — the more context you give, the more accurate the result.
</Callout>

## Document sources

When the AI references a SharePoint document, it shows the source in the response — including the site name and drive. This helps you verify information and navigate back to the original file.

## Disconnecting

To remove a SharePoint connection:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → SharePoint**
2. Find the connected site
3. Click **Disconnect**

The documents are removed from the AI's context immediately.
