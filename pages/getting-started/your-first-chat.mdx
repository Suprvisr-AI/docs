# Your First Chat

Once you've completed onboarding, you're ready to start chatting. Here's how to get the most out of your first conversation.

---

## Starting a conversation

1. Click **New Chat** in the top of the left sidebar, or select an existing conversation
2. Type your question or request in the text field at the bottom
3. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button

The AI responds in real time — you'll see the response stream in as it's generated.

---

## Using example prompts

Not sure what to ask first? Use the **dice icon** in the empty chat state:

- Click the dice to get a random role-specific example prompt
- Click again to get a different one
- Click the prompt text to insert it directly into your input field

These prompts are curated for your role, so they're a good indication of the kinds of tasks the AI handles well.

---

## What to ask

Suprvisr AI works well for:

- **Open-ended questions**: "What's the best way to handle X?"
- **Drafting content**: "Write a one-page spec for this feature"
- **Summarizing**: "Summarize this in 3 bullet points"
- **Debugging help**: "Why would this code fail? [paste code]"
- **Decision support**: "What are the tradeoffs of approach A vs B?"

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro tip**: The more context you give, the better the answer. Instead of "help me with email", try "Help me write a follow-up email to a client who hasn't responded in 2 weeks about a proposal."
</Callout>

---

## Conversation tips

- **You can follow up**: Ask the AI to clarify, expand, or rewrite its response
- **Be specific**: Longer, more specific prompts generally produce better results
- **Paste content**: You can paste code, documents, or text and ask the AI to work with it
- **Start fresh**: Use **New Chat** when switching topics to avoid context confusion
