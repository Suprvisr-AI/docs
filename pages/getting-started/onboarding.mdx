# Onboarding Walkthrough

When you first sign up (or join a new organization), Suprvisr walks you through a quick 3-step setup. This takes about 60 seconds and significantly improves your experience.

---

## The 3-Step Flow

<Steps>

### Step 1 — Welcome & Your Name

The first screen greets you with an animated welcome message: *"Hey there, welcome to Suprvisr AI."*

Below the greeting, you'll see a text field asking: **"What should I call you?"**

Enter your preferred name — this is how the AI will address you in conversation. It doesn't have to match your account name; use whatever feels natural.

Click **Continue** when ready.

### Step 2 — Your Role

Select the role that best describes your work:

| Role | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Developer / Engineer** | Writing code, debugging, technical docs |
| **Product Manager** | Roadmaps, specs, stakeholder updates |
| **Designer / Marketer** | Creative briefs, copy, design feedback |
| **Other** | General use, mixed responsibilities |

Your role determines which example prompts you see and how the AI contextualizes its answers.

### Step 3 — Platform Highlights

A quick tour of what Suprvisr can do:

- **AI Chat** — Ask anything, get instant answers
- **Role-aware responses** — Tailored to your job function
- **Feature Board** — Share feedback directly with the team
- **Personalization** — Update your preferences anytime

Click **Get Started** to enter the app.

</Steps>

---

## Redoing Onboarding

Want to update your name or role? You can redo the onboarding at any time:

1. Click **Settings** in the bottom-left sidebar
2. Go to the **Personalization** tab
3. Click **Redo Onboarding**

<Callout type="info">
  Redoing onboarding resets your role and preferred name. Your chat history is not affected.
</Callout>
