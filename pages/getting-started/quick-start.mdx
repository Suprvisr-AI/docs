# Quick Start

Get from zero to your first AI answer in under 5 minutes.

---

## What you'll need

- An invitation link from your organization admin, or a direct signup URL
- A work email address (personal emails may be restricted by your org settings)

---

## Steps

<Steps>

### Sign up or log in

Navigate to [app.suprvisr.ai](https://app.suprvisr.ai) and create your account, or accept the invite from your team.

### Complete the onboarding

You'll see a 3-step welcome flow:
1. **Tell us your name** — how you'd like the AI to address you
2. **Select your role** — Developer, Product Manager, Designer, or Other
3. **See what's possible** — a quick tour of platform features

Your choices here tailor the AI's behavior to your role.

### Start a new chat

Once you're in, click the **New Chat** button (top of the sidebar) or select an existing conversation. The AI is ready immediately.

### Try an example prompt

Click the **dice icon** in the empty chat state to get a role-specific example prompt. This is a great way to see what kinds of questions the AI handles well.

### Explore

Use the **Feature Board** to submit ideas, check the **Settings** page to update your name and role, or browse this Help Center to learn more.

</Steps>
