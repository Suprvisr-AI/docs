## What is Suprvisr AI?

Suprvisr is a **role-aware AI assistant** built for teams. Unlike generic chatbots, Suprvisr understands your role — developer, designer, PM, or admin — and tailors its responses accordingly.

- **Ask anything** — your work, your tools, your processes
- **Get role-specific answers** — no one-size-fits-all responses
- **Save artifacts** — AI-generated documents and lists are saved as Cards
- **Attach files** — drag and drop spreadsheets, docs, and images for instant analysis
- **Connect SharePoint** — let the AI reference your organization's documents
- **Submit feedback** — use the Feature Board to shape the product
